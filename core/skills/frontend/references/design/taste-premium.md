# Taste Premium

Use for premium, glossy, startup-grade, brand-grade, polished SaaS, marketing, and high-trust product surfaces.

## Goals

- distinctive point of view
- dimensional material
- strong hero moment
- premium typography
- perceptual color ramp
- refined motion
- high trust

## Material requirements

Use a repeatable material recipe:

- layered tint
- subtle gradient
- rim highlight
- soft shadow
- glow or atmospheric light
- texture or depth where appropriate
- hover/active interaction

## Hero requirements

Include one memorable focal object or interaction. Do not make the hero only flat geometric primitives.

## QA failure conditions

- all cards are flat fills
- one brand hex reused everywhere
- default system font despite reference typography
- no hover/active states
- hero has no focal moment
- generic SaaS template output
